About

Created in 2016, img.album serves as a tool for designers, developers and average Jane's to save images found online for use later.

As you have found, downloading images for use later can suck up harddrive space as well as load time. By simply finding the image source URL and saving it to img.album, you're then able to view those images directly on our page, and can then download the images when it's most convenient for you!
